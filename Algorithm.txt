Subway Algorithm:
1) Walk to the nearest exit
2) Turn left
3) Turn right at the first intersection
4) Walk up to the elevator 
5) Enter the elevator
6) Press the first floor button
7) Exit the elevator 
8) Turn left
9) Exit glass door
10) Walk down the stairs 
11) Exit the building
12) Cross the street
13) Turn left 
14) Continue straight down the sidewalk 
15) Subway will be on your right
